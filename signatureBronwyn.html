How to Install Your Email Signature

    Instrucitons for Gmail
  1. Open Gmail and go to Settings ⚙️ > See all settings.
  2. Scroll to the Signature section.
  3. Click Create New and give your signature a name.
  4. Click the Copy Signature button above.
  5. Paste it into Gmail’s signature editor using Ctrl + V or right-click → Paste.
  6. Set it as the default for new emails and/or replies.
  7. Scroll down and click Save Changes.
    Instrucitons for Outlook
  1. Open Outlook and go to File > Options.
  2. In the left pane, click on Mail, then click Signatures.
  3. Click New and give your signature a name.
  4. Click the Copy Signature button above.
  5. Paste it into the signature editor using Ctrl + V or right-click → Paste.
  6. Assign it to new messages and/or replies if desired.
  7. Click OK to save your changes.

    8. Please do this from your desktop and allow changes to sync to your mobile
